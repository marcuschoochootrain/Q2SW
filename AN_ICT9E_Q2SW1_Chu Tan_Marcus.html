Dog Breeds Pictures Description
Siberian Husky

Siberian Huskies make excellent working dogs, as they were bred originally to help the Chukchi people of Siberia hunt more efficiently. But they're sweet, friendly, and loyal dogs too.
Doberman Pinscher

The Doberman Pinscher originated in Germany and quickly gained popularity in other countries for its courage, intelligence, and loyalty.
German Shepherd

German Shepherd dog, breed of working dog developed in Germany from traditional herding and farm dogs.
Chow Chow

Chows are powerful, compactly built dogs standing as high as 20 inches at the shoulder. Dogs this breed usually have a lion's-mane ruff around the head and shoulders.
Labrador Retriever

The Labrador Retriever is the most popular dog breed in the USA. Labs are friendly, outgoing, and high-spirited companions who have more than enough affection to go around for a family looking for a medium-to-large dog.
